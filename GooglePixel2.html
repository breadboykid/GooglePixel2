Google Pixel 2

An informative page about the Google Pixel 2

The Pixel 2 are Android smartphones developed by Google. Manufactured by HTC, there were first announced in a Google event on 4th October 2017 and subsequently released on 19th October 2017.

The phone was release in two versions: Pixel 2, Pixel 2 XL. Please use table below to compare specs. The design consists of an aluminium back with a "premium coating" of plastic. It has a top section made from glass to improve wireless transmissivity.
